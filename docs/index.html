About Me

I am a Computer Science student at the University of Alabama with a minor in Computing Technology and Applications. I have experience in software development, web applications, and automation, with a strong interest in building tools that improve efficiency and solve real-world problems.

I am currently working on an industry-sponsored capstone project where I am developing a web-based Automated Proposal and Fee Calculator using Python, Streamlit, and JSON data structures. This project involves front-end design, user interface development, data management, and generating Word documents programmatically. Through this project, I have gained experience in application structure, UI/UX design, and integrating business logic into software tools.

In addition to software development, I have also worked on automation and data-related projects, including building dashboards, organizing training data, and improving workflows using tools such as Excel, SQL, and scripting. These experiences helped me develop strong problem-solving skills and an understanding of how software can be used to improve business and operational processes.

I am particularly interested in software development, systems, and automation, and I enjoy working on projects where I can design, build, and improve systems that make processes more efficient and easier to use. I am always looking for opportunities to continue learning, gaining experience, and growing.
